User Acquisition & Retention

Scaling to 400,000+ Installs

Successfully launched and scaled 21 Questions to 400,000+ installs across iOS and Android platforms, combining technical excellence with effective growth strategies.

Launch Strategy

Cross-platform deployment using React Native and Expo enabled simultaneous launches on both iOS App Store and Google Play Store, maximizing initial market reach and reducing development overhead.

Firebase Real-Time Features

Integrated Firebase Firestore for real-time syncing and dynamic content delivery:

  • Real-time updates - New content pushed to users without app updates
  • Dynamic card decks - Server-controlled game content for easy iteration
  • Analytics integration - Real-time user behavior tracking for optimization

Growth Channels

Multi-channel acquisition strategy combining:

  • Organic social media traffic from 150K+ follower base
  • App Store Optimization (ASO) for discoverability
  • Word-of-mouth growth from engaging gameplay
  • Strategic content marketing generating 15M+ monthly views

Retention Focus

Firebase-powered real-time features enabled frequent content updates, keeping the app fresh and encouraging repeat usage. The combination of new content delivery and social features helped maintain strong retention metrics as the user base scaled.
