A/B Testing & Monetization

Optimizing Paywalls for 43% MRR Increase

Through systematic A/B testing of different paywall designs and subscription models, I increased Monthly Recurring Revenue (MRR) by 43%.

Testing Methodology

Implemented controlled experiments testing various paywall configurations:

  • Timing - When to show the paywall (immediately, after X sessions, after specific actions)
  • Pricing models - Weekly vs monthly vs annual subscriptions, different price points
  • Presentation - Hard vs soft paywalls, different copy and visual designs
  • Free trial lengths - 3-day, 7-day, or 14-day trial periods

Key Findings

The winning configuration balanced user experience with conversion optimization. Key insights included optimal paywall timing that maximized conversion without hurting retention, and pricing tiers that aligned with user value perception.

Impact

The iterative testing process led to a 43% increase in MRR while maintaining healthy user retention metrics. This data-driven approach to monetization ensured sustainable growth without compromising user experience.
