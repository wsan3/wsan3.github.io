Apple WWDC 2025

1 / 3

June 2025 • Cupertino

Mehul and I went to WWDC 2025 and it was one of those trips I'll remember for a while.

The energy at Apple Park was something else. Being around that many developers all excited about the same things, talking about what they're building, showing each other their apps, it's hard not to feed off that.

The announcements were exciting too. Apple opened up a bunch of new AI capabilities for developers to build with, and we spent a lot of the trip just talking about what we'd want to build now that this stuff is available. It was one of those days where you leave with way more ideas than you came with.

But the moment that really got me was during one of the presentations. Apple listed our app as one of the top apps for the year. I wasn't expecting it at all. We built it from nothing, just the two of us, and seeing it up on that screen in that room was surreal. I looked at Mehul and we were both kind of just in shock for a second.

We took a bunch of photos, ate way too much food at the cafeteria, and drove back hyped. Really glad we went.
