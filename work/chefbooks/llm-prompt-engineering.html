Structured Prompt Design for Recipe Extraction

I designed a structured prompt for recipe extraction with strict JSON schema validation to ensure consistent, machine-readable output.

The prompt handles several tricky edge cases, including parsing ingredients from free-form text (like "2 tbsp olive oil" or "1 medium onion, diced"), estimating macros when nutrition info isn't provided, inferring prep and cook times from natural language, and filtering out non-recipe content entirely.
