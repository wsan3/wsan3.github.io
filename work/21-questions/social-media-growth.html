Social Media Growth

Building a 150K+ Following

Growing the 21 Questions app required building and scaling multiple social media accounts from zero to 150,000+ followers, generating 15M+ monthly views across platforms.

Strategic Content Marketing

The growth strategy focused on creating engaging content that showcased the app's value while encouraging organic sharing. Content was tailored to each platform's unique audience and algorithm preferences, with particular emphasis on TikTok and Instagram Reels.

Audience Engagement

Built community through consistent posting schedules, responding to comments, and creating content based on user feedback. This two-way interaction helped build loyalty and encouraged word-of-mouth promotion.

Key Metrics

  • 150,000+ total followers across platforms
  • 15M+ monthly views
  • Direct impact on app downloads and user acquisition

The social media presence became a core growth channel, driving significant organic traffic and reducing customer acquisition costs compared to traditional paid advertising.
